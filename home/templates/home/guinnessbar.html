About The Guinness Glass Bar

Our signature show-stopper: a visually striking, 4.5-metre-tall centrepiece inspired by the iconic 1970s Guinness tankard-style glass. Some say its profile hints at a rugby ball. Either way, it stops traffic and is picture-perfect as an exhibition stand bar or headline feature for trade shows & exhibitions, weddings, Irish-themed nights and festivals. Engineered for quick builds, high-throughput service and venue compliance, it’s as practical as it is photogenic—perfect for social media and press shots.

With up to six taps, our bartenders keep Guinness on draught and Kilkenny flowing - just one reason the Guinness Glass Bar is perfect for your fair or event:

  • Iconic Silhouette: 40+-year heritage piece that attracts photos and footfall.
  • Event-Ready: deal event bar hire / Irish bar rental; engineered for busy halls.
  • Flexible Deployment: works as a pop-up Irish pub / mobile Irish bar feature, EU-wide.
  • Professional Team: experienced Irish bartenders; tidy builds and seamless breakdowns.

Make your stand the one they talk about. Book the Guinness Glass Bar for premium Irish bar event hire at Messe Frankfurt, Fira Barcelona (Gran Via), Paris Nord Villepinte, Messe Berlin, Messe Düsseldorf—or anywhere across continental Europe (EU)

The Guinness Glass Bar - Specifications
No. of Guests 400 People
No. of Beer Taps: 6
Seating: There is no internal seating
Bar Team: 1 to 5 bar tenders
Height: 4.5m
Size: 4.5m diameter
Head Clearance: Minimum 5.5m from ground to ceiling
Ground: A footprint of 8m diameter is required. It must be flat and solid, and allow for the use of a pallet stacker and pallet truck.
Construction Time: 10 to 18 Hours
Dismantling Time: 7 to 14 Hours
Loading Space: The bar is transported on a 7.5t truck and Sprinter style van. Parking location must be within the vicinity of the bar area, with a smooth surface, to facilitate the movement of pallets, between the two.
Power Required: Minimum 3.5kw/16A
Water In: A fresh clean water connection is required
Water Out: Access is needed to a waste water drain. Waste water consists of H2O and beer waste.
Keg Room: We will construct a keg and storage room. Depending on the size of the event, up to 2 x 8m space is required. It must be located adjacent to the bar.
