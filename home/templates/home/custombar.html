About Custom Bars

When your event needs something specific, our custom bars are built to your brief - delivering premium event bar hire and authentic Irish bar rental without compromise. From a single Guinness tap to a full venue fit-out that feels like a real pub, we'll design, build and staff a solution that fits your footprint, brand and schedule. Perfect for exhibition stand bars, VIP lounges, weddings, private events, festivals and corporate hospitality right across continental EU.

Tailored to perform and built to last the day (or week), every custom bar can include:

  • Footprint & Layout: Bar counter shapes, lengths and back-bar storage configured to your space - indoor or outdoor.
  • Draught & Speed: Guinness on draught plus multiple taps for ales/lager/cider; glassware, refrigeration and queue-smart service.
  • Branding & Media: Bar wraps, signage, lightboxes and optional screens for your logos, promos or sponsor content.
  • Integration: Works as a standalone centrepiece or alongside our pop-up Irish pub / mobile Irish bar formats.

If you can imagine it, we can pour it. Choose Custom Bars by The Pop Up Irish Pub for a solution that looks the part, serves fast, and carries the unmistakable welcome of Ireland - Guinness on draught, professional Irish bartenders, and seamless delivery across continental Europe.
