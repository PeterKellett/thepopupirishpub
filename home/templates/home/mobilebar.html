About The Mobile Bar

Our mobile Irish bar is the most convenient way to bring a real pub - and draught Guinness - to almost any location: compact, on wheels, and fast to deploy. Think plug-and-pour: we arrive, connect the taps, kegs and power, and we’re service-ready in minutes. Ideal for in-booth hospitality at trade shows & exhibitions, full on-stand parties, Irish-themed nights and private events; it also complements external catering at weddings.

Configured to your brief with up to eight draught lines, the mobile Irish bar includes practical features that keep service smooth:

  • Exhibition-Ready: he perfect exhibition stand bar for tastings, demos, networking and after-hours receptions.
  • Wedding-Friendly: great for the full reception—or just welcome drinks or coffee-and-cake time.
  • Practical Logistics: rapid setup, tidy cable runs, compliant power/water handling, multiple draught lines for high throughput.
  • Professional Team: experienced Irish bartenders, clean branding and seamless breakdown—indoors or outdoors, EU-wide.

Wherever you need a crowd-pleasing focal point, choose the mobile Irish bar - authentic pub atmosphere, Guinness on draught, and dependable Irish bar event hire across continental Europe for exhibitions, trade fairs, weddings and private parties.

The Mobile Bar - Specifications
No. of Guests 600 People
No. of Beer Taps: up to 8
Seating: There is no internal seating
Bar Team: 4 to 8 bar tenders
Height: 1m
Size: 1.8 x 0.8m
Head Clearance: Minimum 2.5m from ground to ceiling
Ground: A footprint of 2.8 x 1.8m is required.
Construction Time: 1 to 6 hours
Dismantling Time: 1 to 4 hours
Loading Space: The bar is transported on a Sprinter style van. Parking location must be within the vicinity of the bar area, with a smooth surface, to facilitate the movement of pallets, between the two.
Power Required: Minimum 3.5kw/16A
Water In: The bar does not require a water connection. However, a water connection may still be required depending on the event.
Water Out: As per Water In. Waste water consists of H2O and beer waste.
Keg Room: We will construct a keg and storage room. Depending on the size of the event, up to 2 x 8m space is required. It must be located adjacent to the bar.
