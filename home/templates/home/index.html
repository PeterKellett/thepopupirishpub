About The Pop Up Irish Pub

About Us

Since 1986, we’ve been recreating a little bit of Ireland at events across continental EU - delivering reliable, premium event bar hire and Irish bar event hire that feels like a real Irish pub. Our experienced Irish bartenders serve Guinness on draught and other Irish drinks with pace, professionalism, and a generous dose of Irish banter.

We offer a range of event bar hire and mobile Irish bar formats, support multiple event types, and provide complimentary add-on services:

  • Bar Types: from a single draught Guinness tap to a fully roofed bar for up to ~600 guests, we tailor to your venue.
  • Events: trade shows & exhibitions, weddings, private events, festivals
  • Add-ons: catering, live music, Irish dancers, Djs.

Over the decades we’ve earned our reputation the right way, one excellent pint at a time. So what makes us different?

  • Our People: Our team have spent years behind busy bar counters, so expect fast hands, perfect pours, and natural hosts. Oh, and don’t be surprised if a bartender breaks into song: there’s usually a guitar near the bar, and we’re happy to belt one out.
  • Our Approach: We provide a no hassle service, where we just get on with things, while still paying attention to the finer details.

From Messe Frankfurt to Fira Barcelona (Gran Via), and Düsseldorf to Berlin, choose The Pop Up Irish Pub for quality corporate hospitality and authentic emerald moments.
